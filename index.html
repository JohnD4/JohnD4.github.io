1984

By George Orwell

Introduction

Nineteen Eighty-Four (1984) is a dystopian social science fiction novel by the famous English writer George Orwell. The book was published on 8 June 1949, which is also George Orwell's final book in his lifetime.

Background

The story is set in an imagined future, people in that world lives in a city on the ocean, which is called Oceania. These people, unconciously, are being brainwashed by the government, which makes them forget their life before. The main character is a diligent and skillful rank-and-file worker who is trying to rebel the extreme government, and this book tells the story of his rebellion and the evil of the government holding absolute power.
